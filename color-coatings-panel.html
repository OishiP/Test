Do you provide a warranty with your coatings?
+

We believe in offering the best products with industry-leading service. To stand behind that belief we provide a lifetime warranty for our proprietary polyurea coatings so that you can feel confident in your decision.

Can you coat my damaged concrete?
+

Yes, we can! Our team of trained installers will mend, fill, and grind damaged areas creating a smooth surface for the basecoat. When we’re done, you likely won’t even notice old, damaged areas.

How long does it take to complete a garage coatings project?
+

Our trained teams of 2-4 members will complete your job in as little as one day, which means you can be back on your floor within 24 hours.

Will my garage coating scratch?
+

The concrete coating industry has widely adopted the latest polyurea technology, and the benefits to epoxy are widely known. However, here at Renuity we didn’t stop there. We sought out a solution that provides an even tougher, more durable finish with increased protection from scratching and abrasion and developed a proprietary co-polymer additive that does just that.
